Custom 3D Printing Service

Need something printed, replaced, or customized? I offer custom 3D printing from Hot Springs, Arkansas. Send an STL file or describe what you need — I'll help you get a clean, functional print.

What I Can Print

  • Replacement parts
  • Prototypes and test-fit parts
  • Brackets, mounts, and small hardware
  • Organizers, holders, and desk tools
  • Keychains, tags, and small gifts
  • Articulated toys and display pieces

How It Works

  1. Send your file or idea
  2. I confirm size, material, and timeline
  3. I print, test, and finish the part
  4. You approve and I ship or arrange pickup

Materials and Quality

Most prints are PLA for strong, clean everyday parts. I can recommend settings and materials based on your use case. Every print is quality checked before delivery.

Pricing

Pricing is by quote based on size, material, and print time. Small items are often quick and affordable. Complex or functional parts may require test prints.
