How Much Does a Small Business Website Cost?

One of the most common questions business owners ask is how much a professional website actually costs. The answer varies depending on complexity, but understanding the typical ranges can help you plan the right investment for your business.

Typical Website Price Ranges

  • DIY builders (Wix, Squarespace): $0 – $40/month
  • Freelance designers: $800 – $3,000
  • Small web design agencies: $3,000 – $10,000+
  • Large agencies: $10,000 – $50,000+

What Affects Website Cost?

  • Number of pages
  • Custom design work
  • Mobile responsiveness
  • Search engine optimization
  • Content and images
  • Hosting and ongoing support
