Pricing FAQ

What\'s the difference between the $400 and $900 setups?

The $400 Starter Setup is a single-page website — homepage with hours, service description, contact form, and click-to-call. The $900 Business Setup is 3–5 pages, including a dedicated service page (or pages), stronger calls-to-action throughout, Google Business Profile optimization, review system setup, and baseline SEO structure. Most small businesses end up needing the Business Setup.

Is hosting required?

Not strictly. You can host the site elsewhere if you have a preferred host. But the $89/month Hosting and Support plan includes regular backups, security monitoring, minor content updates (hours changes, holiday updates, photo swaps), and direct support — basically the difference between owning a website and having to manage one. Most clients opt in. See website maintenance for full scope.

How does payment work?

Projects begin with a 50% deposit. The remaining balance is due before launch. You receive a secure Stripe payment link by email. Before any payment, you can review the simple project agreement. No surprises.

Are there hidden fees?

No. The price you see is the price you pay. Domain registration ($12–20/year) and any premium third-party tools (paid online ordering, advanced reservation systems) are passed through at cost with no markup. Everything else is included.

Can I cancel monthly hosting & support?

Yes — month-to-month, cancel any time. If you cancel, we help you migrate to another host. You keep your domain, your files, and your content. No lock-in.

Do you offer discounts for churches or nonprofits?

Yes. Churches qualify for the $149/month Church Care Plan which adds weekly sign graphic content on top of hosting. Other registered nonprofits get a discount on the initial build — ask Topher directly.

Different question? Ask Topher directly — typical reply within a few hours during the workday.
