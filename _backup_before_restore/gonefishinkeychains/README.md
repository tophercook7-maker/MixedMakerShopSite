# GoneFishin Keychains Website

A beautiful, modern website for your fishing lure keychain business that matches the MixedMakerShop style with a fishing theme.

## What's Included

### 🎨 **Website** (`index.html`)
- **Home page** with hero section, product highlights, and clear call-to-actions
- **Products section** with placeholders for your 3 product types:
  - Vintage Lure Keychains ($18.99)
  - Regular Lure Keychains ($12.99)
  - 3D Printed Keychains ($8.99)
- **About section** telling your story and why customers should choose you
- **Contact section** with your email, phone, and address
- **Fully responsive** - looks great on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **Fishing theme** with water colors, teals, and greens matching MixedMakerShop palette

### 🐟 **Logo** (`logo.svg`)
- Beautiful circular logo with fish, fishing hook, and water theme
- Perfect for stickers, business cards, and branding
- Uses your color palette (green, teal, orange)
- Includes "GFK" initials in the design

### 🔗 **Integration**
- Updated MixedMakerShop.com to link to your new site
- "Visit Website →" button in the ventures section

## Product Placeholders

The website has 3 product cards ready for your photos. Each has:
- Image placeholder (you'll replace with real photos)
- Product title
- Description
- Price
- Category badge

Just replace the placeholder `<div class="product-image"></div>` with:
```html
<img src="your-photo.jpg" alt="Vintage Lure Keychain" class="product-image" />
```
