# Product Images Directory

Add your 3D print example photos here!

## Quick Start

1. **Download free images** from these sites:
   - **Pixabay**: https://pixabay.com/images/search/3d%20print/
   - **Unsplash**: https://unsplash.com/s/photos/3d-printed
   - **Pexels**: https://www.pexels.com/search/3d%20printing/

2. **Search terms to use**:
   - "3d printed fidget" or "fidget toy"
   - "cable organizer" or "desk organizer"
   - "3d printed keychain" or "custom keychain"
   - "3d printed parts" or "printed components"

3. **Save images with these exact names** in this folder:
   - `keychain-vintage.jpg` (or .png)
   - `fidget.jpg` (or .png)
   - `cable-anchors.jpg` (or .png)
   - `custom-parts.jpg` (or .png)

## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Recommended size**: 800x800px to 1200x1200px (square works best)
- **File size**: Keep under 500KB for fast loading
- **Aspect ratio**: Square (1:1) works best - images will be cropped to square automatically

## Tips for Finding Good Images

- ✅ Look for images with clean, simple backgrounds
- ✅ White or neutral backgrounds work best with your dark green theme
- ✅ Show the product clearly from a good angle
- ✅ Multiple items in one photo can work well
- ✅ Images will automatically hide if they don't exist (graceful fallback)
- ❌ Avoid busy backgrounds or cluttered images
- ❌ Avoid images with watermarks or text overlays

## After Adding Images

1. Save the images in this folder (`/images/products/`)
2. Use the exact filenames listed above
3. Refresh your browser - images should appear automatically!
4. If images don't show, check:
   - File names match exactly (case-sensitive)
   - File extensions (.jpg or .png)
   - Images are in the correct folder
