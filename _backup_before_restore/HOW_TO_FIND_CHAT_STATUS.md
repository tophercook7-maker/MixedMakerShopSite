# How to Find Your Chat Status - Step by Step

## 📍 Exact Steps to Find Your Chat Status

### Step 1: Go to Google Search
1. Open your browser
2. Go to: **https://www.google.com**
3. Search for: **"MixedMakerShop"**

### Step 2: Find "Edit profile" Button
1. Look for the section: **"Your business on Google"**
2. You'll see a grid of buttons/icons
3. Click the button that says: **"Edit profile"** (it has a pencil icon)

### Step 3: Click "Contact" Tab
1. A popup/dialog will open showing "Business information"
2. At the top, you'll see tabs: **About | Contact | Location | Hours | More**
3. Click on the **"Contact"** tab

### Step 4: Find "Chat" Section
1. Scroll down in the Contact tab
2. Look for a section labeled **"Chat"**
3. This is where you'll see:
   - **CURRENT** - What's active now
   - **PENDING** - What's waiting for approval

---

## 🎯 What You Should See

In the **Chat** section, you'll see:

**CURRENT:**
- `sms:+15015758017` (your SMS number)

**PENDING:**
- `https://wa.me/15015758017` (WhatsApp)
- `sms:+15015758017` (SMS)

**Status Banner:**
- Yellow banner saying: "Your edit is pending. It usually takes up to 10 minutes to be reviewed."

## 🔗 Direct Link (Try This!)

If the steps above don't work, try this direct link:

**Copy and paste this into your browser:**
```
https://www.google.com/search?q=MixedMakerShop#mpd=editprofile/info/contact
```

This should take you directly to the Contact tab where you can see the Chat section.

## 📱 Alternative: Check Your Public Business Profile

1. Go to: **https://www.google.com/maps**
2. Search for: **"MixedMakerShop"**
3. Click on your business listing
4. Look for a **"Message"** button
   - If you see it = Chat is working!
   - If you don't see it = Still pending
