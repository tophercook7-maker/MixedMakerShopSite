# Google Business Profile Description Update - Step by Step

## 🎯 Quick Update Steps

Here's exactly how to add the 3D print disclosure to your Google Business Profile description:

---

## 📝 Step 1: Open Your Google Business Profile

**Option A - From Google Search:**
1. Go to: https://www.google.com/search?q=MixedMakerShop
2. Look for "Your business on Google" section
3. Click **"Edit profile"** button (pencil icon)

**Option B - Direct Link:**
1. Go to: https://business.google.com
2. Sign in if needed
3. Select your business: **MixedMakerShop**

---

## 📝 Step 2: Go to About/Description Section

1. In the edit dialog, click the **"About"** tab (or "Business information" → "About")
2. Find the **"Description"** or **"From the business"** field
3. Click **"Edit"** next to it

---

## 📝 Step 3: Update Your Description

### Current Description (What you likely have):
```
MixedMakerShop is a small studio that turns ideas into things you can hold, use, and ship. We build custom websites, e-commerce platforms, and 3D-printed products for small businesses and creators.
```

### Updated Description (With Disclosure):
```
MixedMakerShop is a small studio that turns ideas into things you can hold, use, and ship. We build custom websites, e-commerce platforms, and 3D-printed products for small businesses and creators.

Note: The 3D printed items we offer are from our extensive catalog of available designs. While these designs are not our original creations, we have access to thousands of 3D print designs and can produce any of them for you, customized to your size, color, and material preferences.
```

### OR - Shorter Version:
```
MixedMakerShop provides custom 3D printing services and website building for small businesses. We offer 3D printing from our extensive catalog—while these designs are not our original creations, we have access to thousands of 3D print designs and can produce any of them customized to your preferences. We also build responsive websites and provide web design services.

From ideas to things you can hold—3D creations, fidgets, and fixes, layer by layer.
```

---

## 📝 Step 4: Copy & Paste This Text

**Full Description with Disclosure:**
```
MixedMakerShop is a small studio that turns ideas into things you can hold, use, and ship. We build custom websites, e-commerce platforms, and 3D-printed products for small businesses and creators.

Note: The 3D printed items we offer are from our extensive catalog of available designs. While these designs are not our original creations, we have access to thousands of 3D print designs and can produce any of them for you, customized to your size, color, and material preferences.
```

---

## ✅ Step 5: Save

1. Click **"Save"** or **"Apply"** button
2. Wait for Google to review (usually instant, sometimes a few minutes)
3. Done! ✅

---

## 🔍 Alternative: If You Can't Find Description Field

### Try These Locations:
1. **Business information** → **About** tab → **Description**
2. **Info** → **From the business** section
3. **Edit profile** → **About** → **Business description**
4. Look for a text area that says "Describe your business" or "Tell customers about your business"

---

## 📸 If You Have Photos of 3D Prints

For each photo:
1. Go to **"Photos"** section
2. Click on a 3D print photo
3. Click **"Edit"** or add caption
4. Add: `Example from our 3D print catalog. This design is not our original creation, but we can produce it customized to your preferences.`
