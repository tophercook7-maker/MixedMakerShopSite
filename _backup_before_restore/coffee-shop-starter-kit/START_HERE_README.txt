START HERE (READ THIS FIRST)
============================

You only need to edit TWO files:
1) EDIT_BASICS.txt  (name, phone, address, hours)
2) EDIT_MENU.txt    (menu items and prices)

You do NOT need to understand "code."
You will only change words and numbers.

WHAT TO USE TO EDIT
-------------------
Windows: Notepad
Mac: TextEdit

HOW TO EDIT
-----------
1) Open EDIT_BASICS.txt in Notepad/TextEdit
2) Change the info
3) Save

4) Open EDIT_MENU.txt
5) Change menu items/prices
6) Save

NEXT STEP (put your info into the website)
------------------------------------------
Open index.html in a browser.

Then open index.html in Notepad/TextEdit too.
Use Find (Ctrl+F on Windows, Command+F on Mac) and search for:

__BUSINESS_NAME__
__TAGLINE__
__PHONE__
__EMAIL__
__ADDRESS1__
__ADDRESS2__
__HOURS__

Replace those placeholders with your info from EDIT_BASICS.txt.

For the menu: Open index.html and find the Menu, Pastries, and Snacks sections.
Update those items and prices using EDIT_MENU.txt as your reference.

PREVIEW
-------
Double-click index.html to preview.

PUBLISH (NETLIFY)
-----------------
1) Go to netlify.com and create a free account
2) Drag-and-drop this entire folder
3) Your website is live

CONTACT FORM
------------
This kit includes a contact form for Netlify.
After publishing on Netlify, form submissions show inside your Netlify dashboard.
