# Complete Guide: Update Google Business Profile with 3D Print Disclosure

## 🎯 What We're Doing

Adding a disclosure to your Google Business Profile description that clarifies the 3D print designs aren't your original creations, but you have access to thousands of designs.

---

## 📝 Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Open Your Google Business Profile

**Method A - From Google Search (Easiest):**
1. Go to: **https://www.google.com**
2. Search for: **"MixedMakerShop"**
3. Look for the section: **"Your business on Google"**
4. Click the button: **"Edit profile"** (pencil icon)

**Method B - Direct Link:**
1. Go to: **https://business.google.com**
2. Sign in if needed
3. Click on: **"MixedMakerShop"** from your business list
4. Click: **"Edit profile"** or look for **"Info"** in the left sidebar

---

### Step 2: Find the Description Field

1. In the edit dialog, you'll see tabs at the top: **About | Contact | Location | Hours | More**
2. Click on the **"About"** tab
3. Look for a section called:
   - **"Description"** OR
   - **"From the business"** OR
   - **"Business description"** OR
   - **"About your business"**
4. Click **"Edit"** next to that section

---

### Step 3: Update Your Description

**Copy this entire text and paste it into the description field:**

```
MixedMakerShop is a small studio that turns ideas into things you can hold, use, and ship. We build custom websites, e-commerce platforms, and 3D-printed products for small businesses and creators.

Note: The 3D printed items we offer are from our extensive catalog of available designs. While these designs are not our original creations, we have access to thousands of 3D print designs and can produce any of them for you, customized to your size, color, and material preferences.
```

**OR if you want a shorter version:**

```
MixedMakerShop provides custom 3D printing services and website building for small businesses. We offer 3D printing from our extensive catalog—while these designs are not our original creations, we have access to thousands of 3D print designs and can produce any of them customized to your preferences. We also build responsive websites and provide web design services.

From ideas to things you can hold—3D creations, fidgets, and fixes, layer by layer.
```

---

### Step 4: Save Your Changes

1. Click the **"Save"** or **"Apply"** button
2. Wait a moment for Google to save
3. You might see a message: "Your edit is pending" - that's normal
4. It usually takes a few minutes to appear on your public listing

---

## ✅ Verification

After saving, check your public listing:

1. Go to: **https://www.google.com/search?q=MixedMakerShop**
2. Click on your business listing (the one with your logo)
3. Scroll to the **"About"** section
4. You should see your updated description with the disclosure

---

## 📸 If You Have 3D Print Photos

### For Each Photo:

1. Go to **"Photos"** section in your Google Business Profile
2. Click on a 3D print photo
3. Click **"Edit"** or add a caption
4. Add this caption:
   ```
   Example from our 3D print catalog. This design is not our original creation, but we can produce it customized to your preferences. We have access to thousands of 3D print designs!
   ```

## 🎯 Quick Reference

**What to add:**
- Disclosure about 3D print designs not being original
- Statement that you have access to thousands of designs
- Note that items can be customized

**Where to add it:**
- Main business description (most important)
- Photo captions (if you upload 3D print photos)
- Posts/updates (if you post about 3D prints)

**Text to use:**
```
Note: The 3D printed items we offer are from our extensive catalog of available designs. While these designs are not our original creations, we have access to thousands of 3D print designs and can produce any of them for you, customized to your size, color, and material preferences.
```

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**Can't find the description field?**
- Try: Edit profile → About tab → Look for "Description" or "From the business"
- Or: Info → About → Description

**Description field is read-only?**
- Make sure you're logged in as a manager/owner
- Try refreshing the page
- Check if there's a pending edit that needs approval first

**Changes not showing?**
- Wait 5-10 minutes for Google to process
- Clear your browser cache
- Check the public listing (not just the edit view)
