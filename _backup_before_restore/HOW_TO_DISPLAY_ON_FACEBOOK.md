# How to Display Your Website on Facebook - Complete Guide

## 🎯 Quick Summary
There are **3 main ways** to display your website on Facebook:
1. **Share the link** in a post (requires Open Graph tags - ✅ DONE!)
2. **Create a Facebook Page** and link your website
3. **Use Facebook Posts** to drive traffic to your site

---

## ✅ Step 1: Verify Open Graph Tags (DONE!)

I've already added Open Graph meta tags to your `index.html`. These tags tell Facebook:
- What title to show
- What description to display
- What image to use (your banner.svg)
- How to format the preview

**Your site is now ready for Facebook sharing!**

---

## 📱 Step 2: How to Share Your Website Link on Facebook

### Method A: Share from Your Personal Profile

1. **Go to Facebook** and click "What's on your mind?" (or "Create Post")
2. **Paste your website URL**: `https://mixedmakershop.com`
3. **Wait a moment** - Facebook will fetch a preview automatically
4. **Add your post text** above the preview
5. **Click "Post"**

**Example Post Text:**
```
🌐 Check out MixedMakerShop! 

We build custom websites, 3D prints, and digital tools. 

See our work: GoneFishin Keychains & Kelsey'sKustom Kreations

Visit: mixedmakershop.com

#CustomWebsites #3DPrinting #WebDevelopment
```

### Method B: Share on Your Facebook Business Page

1. **Go to your Facebook Page** (or create one - see Step 3)
2. **Click "Create Post"** or "What's on your mind?"
3. **Paste your URL**: `https://mixedmakershop.com`
4. **Facebook will show a preview** with image, title, and description
5. **Add your message** and click "Post"

## 🏢 Step 3: Create a Facebook Business Page (If You Don't Have One)

### Quick Setup (5 minutes)

1. **Go to**: https://www.facebook.com/pages/create
2. **Choose**: "Business or Brand"
3. **Fill in**:
   - **Page Name**: `MixedMakerShop`
   - **Category**: `Business Service` or `Gift Shop`
   - Click **"Create Page"**

4. **Add Profile Picture**:
   - Upload your `m3-logo.svg` (convert to PNG first)
   - Size: 170 x 170 pixels
   - Square image works best

5. **Add Cover Photo**:
   - Use the flyer you created: `facebook-flyer.html`
   - Screenshot it at 1640 x 859 pixels
   - Or create one with your brand colors

6. **Fill in About Section**:
   - **Short Description**: `A tiny studio building custom websites, 3D prints, and digital tools.`
   - **Website**: `https://mixedmakershop.com`
   - **Description**: 
     ```
     MixedMakerShop – a tiny studio turning ideas into things you can hold, use and ship.
     
     We build:
     • Custom websites (see GoneFishin Keychains & Kelsey'sKustom Kreations)
     • Personalized 3D prints (dragons, keychains, figurines)
     • Digital tools & small ventures
     
     Visit: mixedmakershop.com
     ```

7. **Add Call-to-Action Button**:
   - Click **"+ Add a Button"** (below cover photo)
   - Choose: **"Contact Us"** or **"Learn More"**
   - Link to: `https://mixedmakershop.com`

## 🔧 Step 5: Test Your Facebook Preview

### Using Facebook's Sharing Debugger (IMPORTANT!)

1. **Go to**: https://developers.facebook.com/tools/debug/
2. **Enter your URL**: `https://mixedmakershop.com`
3. **Click "Debug"**
4. **Review the preview** - this is exactly how it will look when shared
5. **Click "Scrape Again"** to refresh if you made changes

**What to check:**
- ✅ Image appears correctly
- ✅ Title is correct
- ✅ Description is accurate
- ✅ URL is correct

## 🎯 Step 7: Best Practices for Facebook Sharing

### ✅ DO:
- **Always include your URL** in posts
- **Use the link preview** Facebook generates
- **Post regularly** (2-3 times per week)
- **Use engaging images** with your posts
- **Include a clear call-to-action** (Visit site, Get quote, etc.)
- **Tag your page** if posting from personal profile
- **Use relevant hashtags**: #CustomWebsites #3DPrinting #WebDevelopment

### ❌ DON'T:
- Don't just paste URL without context
- Don't post too frequently (spam)
- Don't use all caps or excessive emojis
- Don't forget to engage with comments

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Problem: Preview doesn't show correctly
**Solution**: 
1. Use Facebook Sharing Debugger
2. Click "Scrape Again" to refresh
3. Check that Open Graph tags are correct
4. Ensure image URL is accessible (full https:// URL)

### Problem: Image doesn't appear
**Solution**:
- Use absolute URL (https://mixedmakershop.com/image.jpg)
- Image should be at least 600 x 315 pixels
- Check image is accessible (not blocked)

### Problem: Description is wrong
**Solution**:
- Update `og:description` meta tag
- Use Facebook Debugger to refresh
- Clear Facebook's cache by scraping again
