# Google Business Profile - 3D Print Disclosure Update

## ✅ Yes, Your Google Business Profile Needs This Info Too!

If your Google Business Profile mentions 3D printing services or shows 3D print images, you should add a disclosure to clarify that the designs aren't your original creations.

---

## 📍 Where to Add the Disclosure

### Option 1: In the Business Description (Recommended)
Add it to your "About" or "Description" section.

### Option 2: In Posts/Updates
If you post photos of 3D prints, include the disclosure in the post caption.

### Option 3: In Photo Captions
If you upload 3D print photos, add the disclosure in each photo's caption.

---

## 📝 How to Update Your Google Business Profile

### Step 1: Go to Edit Profile
1. Search "MixedMakerShop" on Google
2. Click **"Edit profile"** button
3. Or go to: https://business.google.com

### Step 2: Edit the Description
1. Click **"About"** tab (or "Business information")
2. Find **"Description"** or **"From the business"** section
3. Click **"Edit"**

### Step 3: Add the Disclosure
Add this to your description (you can add it at the end):

**Option A - Short Version:**
```
Note: The 3D printed items we offer are from our extensive catalog of available designs. While these designs are not our original creations, we have access to thousands of 3D print designs and can produce any of them for you, customized to your size, color, and material preferences.
```

**Option B - Integrated into Description:**
Add this sentence to your existing description:
```
We offer custom 3D printing services from our extensive catalog of available designs. While these designs are not our original creations, we have access to thousands of 3D print designs and can produce any of them customized to your preferences.
```

**Option C - Full Updated Description:**
```
MixedMakerShop provides custom 3D printing services and website building for small businesses. We offer 3D printing from our extensive catalog of available designs—while these designs are not our original creations, we have access to thousands of 3D print designs and can produce any of them for you, customized to your size, color, and material preferences. We also build responsive websites and provide web design services for small businesses.

From ideas to things you can hold—3D creations, fidgets, and fixes, layer by layer.
```

### Step 4: Save
Click **"Save"** or **"Apply"**

---

## 📸 If You Have 3D Print Photos

### For Each Photo:
1. Go to **"Photos"** section
2. Click on a 3D print photo
3. Click **"Edit"** or add a caption
4. Add this to the caption:
   ```
   Example from our 3D print catalog. This design is not our original creation, but we can produce it for you in your preferred size, color, and material. We have access to thousands of 3D print designs!
   ```

### For Posts/Updates:
If you create posts showing 3D prints, include in the post text:
```
These are examples from our 3D print catalog. While these designs aren't our original creations, we have access to thousands of designs and can produce any of them customized to your preferences!
```

## 🎯 Recommended Approach

**Best practice:** Add a brief disclosure to your main business description, then you don't need to repeat it in every photo/post.

**Example Description with Disclosure:**
```
MixedMakerShop – Custom 3D printing services and website building for small businesses.

We offer 3D printing from our extensive catalog of available designs. While these designs are not our original creations, we have access to thousands of 3D print designs and can produce any of them customized to your size, color, and material preferences.

We also build responsive websites and provide web design services for small businesses.

From ideas to things you can hold—3D creations, fidgets, and fixes, layer by layer.

Visit mixedmakershop.com
```

## 💡 Why This Matters

- **Transparency**: Customers know what to expect
- **Legal clarity**: Avoids confusion about design ownership
- **Sets expectations**: Customers understand you're a printing service, not a design studio
- **Professional**: Shows you're clear about your services
