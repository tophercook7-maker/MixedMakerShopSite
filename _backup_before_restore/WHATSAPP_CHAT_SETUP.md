### Step 2: Set Up in Google Business Profile

1. **Go to**: Google Search → "MixedMakerShop" → "Edit profile"
2. **Click**: "Contact" tab
3. **Scroll to**: "Chat" section
4. **In the dropdown**: Select **"WhatsApp"** (instead of "Google Messages" or "SMS")
5. **Paste your WhatsApp URL**: `https://wa.me/15015758017`
6. **Click**: "Save" or "Apply"
7. **Wait for approval** (usually quick, 10 minutes to a few hours)

### Step 3: Test It!

1. Search "MixedMakerShop" on Google
2. Look for the "Message" button on your Business Profile
3. Click it - it should open WhatsApp with your business number pre-filled
4. Customers can start chatting directly!

## 💡 WhatsApp Business Features

If you set up WhatsApp Business (vs regular WhatsApp), you get:

- ✅ **Business Profile** - Show your business info, hours, website
- ✅ **Quick Replies** - Pre-written responses for common questions
- ✅ **Labels** - Organize conversations
- ✅ **Catalog** - Show products/services in chat
- ✅ **Business Hours** - Auto-replies during off-hours
- ✅ **Away Messages** - Set when you're not available

---

## 🆚 WhatsApp vs SMS (Google Messages)

### Choose **WhatsApp** if:
- ✅ You have international customers
- ✅ You already use WhatsApp Business
- ✅ You want rich features (catalogs, quick replies)
- ✅ Customers in your area prefer WhatsApp

### Choose **SMS (Google Messages)** if:
- ✅ Most customers are in the US
- ✅ You prefer simple text messaging
- ✅ You don't want another app to manage
- ✅ You want messages on your regular phone number
