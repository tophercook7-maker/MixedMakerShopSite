Why local 3D printing makes sense

For small runs — 1, 5, 50, or even a couple hundred pieces — 3D printing is dramatically cheaper than traditional manufacturing. There's no mold to tool. No minimum order quantity. The same setup that prints one prints ten.

For replacement parts, it's often the only option. The bracket that broke on your dishwasher isn't in stock anywhere because it was discontinued in 2018. We can model it from a photo or measurements, print a replacement, and you're back in business for the cost of a fast-food lunch.

For personalized gifts, it's the difference between "I bought this" and "I had this made." A dragon with your kid's name on its tail isn't something Amazon stocks. We print it. You give it. They keep it forever.

Hot Springs locals: you can pick up directly. Anywhere else in the country: we ship, usually arriving within a week of approval. Either way, you're talking directly to GiGi — no order portal, no middleman.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a custom 3D print cost?

Pricing is quote-based — depending on size, color, material, and how much finishing the piece needs. Small keychains and brackets often run $5–$15. Mid-size figures and parts $15–$45. Large or detailed pieces (multi-day prints) are quoted individually. We always quote before printing.

What file formats do you accept?

STL is the gold standard. We also accept STEP, OBJ, and 3MF. Don't have a file? Send a photo, a sketch, the broken part, or measurements — we can model the design from there for an additional fee (usually $20–$80 depending on complexity).

How long does a typical print take?

Small items (under 4 hours of print time) usually ship within 3–5 business days of quote approval. Larger or batch orders take longer and we'll quote a turnaround time up front. Rush jobs are sometimes possible for an additional fee — ask.

What materials do you print with?

PLA is the standard — strong, color-rich, finishes well, and prints reliably. We stock multiple colors. For parts that need higher heat tolerance, flexibility, or specific properties, we can source PETG, TPU, or other filaments on request — we'll discuss what the part actually needs to do.

Do you do bulk orders or wholesale?

Yes. Branded keychains for a giveaway, 50 nametags for a conference, parts for an Etsy shop — bulk pricing kicks in around 25 units. Send the details and we'll quote.
