FAQ

What\'s the difference between Things We\'ve Built and Website Samples?

Things We\'ve Built is the broader hub — it covers everything the studio has shipped across all categories (client websites, 3D printing, AI tools, experiments, ventures). Website Samples is the curated portfolio of just client websites with full demos you can click through.

Can I commission something from one of these categories?

Yes for Client Websites, 3D Printing, Business Tools, and AI Tools — those are the active service categories with public pages and pricing. Henry AI and StrainSpotter are studio projects, not services, so they\'re not for sale (but reach out if you want to collaborate or get build updates).

Are the experiments and future ventures available?

Most experiments are pre-release or internal-only. If something on that list specifically interests you, get in touch — early-stage collaborators sometimes shape what we ship next. Future ventures are sketches and plans; some will become real products, some won\'t.

How often does this page update?

Whenever something ships or moves forward. The hub is intentionally rolling — what\'s here today is not all of it, and what\'s missing today might be on the bench. We add new entries as projects reach a public-shareable state.
