What every coffee shop site needs

Visual-first, mobile-first, hand-on-wheel friendly. These are the must-haves.

01 · Menu

Drink menu with prices

Espresso, drip, pour-over, cold drinks, seasonal specials, syrups. Prices visible. No menu PDF download.

Daily hours + holiday hours

Big, mobile-readable. Special hours for Hot Springs marathon weekend, holidays, weather closures. Easy to update.

Why coffee shops can't just lean on social

A lot of coffee shop owners say "we just use Instagram." That works for the existing customer base — but new visitors don't see your Instagram unless you advertise. They search "coffee near me" or "best coffee Hot Springs" on Google, and Google shows them a list of shops with websites.

If you're not on that list — or you're on it with a half-finished site that loads slowly — they go to the next shop. The shop they would have liked yours better, but you never got the chance.

Hot Springs has a year-round tourist base — Bathhouse Row, the lake, marathon weekend, racing season. These visitors aren't scrolling local Instagram accounts. They're Googling at 8:30 AM trying to find a real coffee shop before heading out. The shops that show up clearly online get that business.

A coffee shop website doesn't replace Instagram. It does the job Instagram can't — be the answer to "where should I go right now."

Frequently asked questions

Can I sell bags of coffee online?

Yes. We hook up Shopify, Square, or a simple Stripe-based shop depending on volume. If you're shipping coffee, we wire shipping rates, weights, and roast-date displays.

Do you take photos for the site?

Not directly, but we can refer a local photographer. Most shops have enough usable photos on their phone if we cull the good ones. We'll tell you straight if a phone shoot would be worth the half-day.

How long does a coffee shop site take?

Most projects are live within 5–10 business days once we have your menu, hours, and photos. We launch the basics first; iterate on photo galleries and Instagram embeds after.

Do you also handle Instagram posting?

Posting only, yes. See Social Media Takeover. We do not reply to comments or DMs as you — that voice stays yours.

What about online ordering?

We integrate Toast Online Ordering, Square Online, ChowNow, or whatever you use. Customer experience stays seamless — they don't feel like they got routed to a third party.
