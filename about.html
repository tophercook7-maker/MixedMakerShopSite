How we work

A few rules we picked up the hard way. They shape what working with us actually looks like.

01 · No layers

You talk to the person doing the work

First call, mid-project change, final review — same person. No tickets, no account managers, no "let me check with the team."

02 · Start small

Mockup or quote first, not scoping decks

A free homepage mockup is faster than a 30-minute discovery call. You react to a real layout. Or for clear projects, a written quote within 24 hours.

03 · Honest pricing

Written quotes, no surprise invoices

The price you see in the quote is the price you pay. Domain, paid integrations, anything else — passed through at cost. See pricing.

04 · You own it

Your domain, your hosting, your files

No proprietary platform lock-in. If you stop working with us — for any reason — the site keeps running. The files are yours.

05 · Sized small

Projects sized for small businesses

No $5,000 sites for a one-truck lawn care business. No upsells for things you do not need. We build what helps. We skip what does not.

06 · Local first

Tuned for the customers you actually have

We live in Hot Springs. We know the local search habits, the geography customers describe in their own words, the Saturday-morning patterns. The site reflects that.

About the studio · FAQ

Who runs MixedMakerShop?

Two people: Topher Cook (founder — handles web design, local SEO, AI tools, and computer help) and GiGi (co-founder — handles 3D printing through GiGi\'s Print Shop). Both based in Hot Springs, Arkansas. When you reach out, you\'re talking to one of us directly — no account managers.

Are you a freelancer or an agency?

Neither, exactly. We call it a studio. Smaller than an agency (no account managers, no PM layer, no hand-offs), more structured than a typical freelancer (clear pricing, written agreement, ongoing support plans). Intentionally boutique.

Why call it MixedMakerShop?

Because we make a mix of things. Web design, software-side tools, and physical 3D-printed objects all live under the same roof — driven by the same standards. The brand is the umbrella; the work underneath is what we actually sell.

Do I have to be in Hot Springs to work with you?

No. The studio is based in Hot Springs, Arkansas, and a lot of work happens in person locally. But most projects are remote-friendly. Clients across Arkansas and nationwide are welcome — contact us for the easiest way to start.

What makes you different from a national web design agency?

Three things. First, you talk to the person doing the work. Second, the work is sized for small businesses, not Fortune 500 budgets. Third, we live where many of our clients live — we know what a "Hot Springs Saturday" looks like and tune the site for it.

Different question? Ask us directly — typical reply within a few hours during the workday.
