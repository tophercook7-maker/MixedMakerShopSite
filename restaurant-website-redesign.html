What makes a redesign different from a new site

A new site starts from zero — no rankings, no inbound links, no Google footprint. That's fine if you're a new restaurant. It's expensive if you're an established one. Years of Google ranking equity, hard-earned reviews, and inbound links from local sites would all be lost if we just built somewhere new and pointed the domain.

A redesign keeps all of that. We map every existing URL to the new structure. We set up redirects for any URL that has to change. Google sees the same domain with a faster, better site — and your rankings actually improve as a result of the speed and structure work.

The work is more careful than a new build. The payoff is that you don't start over.

For Hot Springs restaurants specifically, "best [cuisine] in Hot Springs" and "[restaurant name] hours" are the rankings worth protecting. A bad redesign loses them. A careful redesign moves them up.

Frequently asked questions

Will I lose my Google search rankings during the redesign?

No — that's the whole point. We map your existing URLs to the new structure, set up 301 redirects for anything that has to change, and submit the new sitemap to Google. Rankings typically improve from the speed and structure work, not drop.

Do I have to switch hosting?

Not necessarily. If your current host is reasonably fast, we can rebuild on top of it. If it's painfully slow we'll recommend a migration — usually to something cheaper than what you're paying now. Your call either way.

Can I keep my existing domain name?

Yes. The domain stays. The URL structure mostly stays. Customers and Google see the same address.

How long does a redesign take?

Most restaurant redesigns ship in 7–14 business days from project start, depending on complexity. We launch the basics first and iterate on integrations.

How much does a restaurant redesign cost?

Starts at $600 for single-location redesigns with menu modernization. Full redesigns with new POS integration, online ordering, and reservations typically run $900–$1,800. See pricing.
