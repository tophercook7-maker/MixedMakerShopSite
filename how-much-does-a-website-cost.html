How Much Does a Small Business Website Cost?

One of the most common questions business owners ask is how much a professional website actually costs. The answer varies depending on complexity, but understanding the typical ranges can help you plan the right investment.

Typical Website Price Ranges

  • DIY builders (Wix, Squarespace): $0 – $40/month
  • Freelance designers: $800 – $3,000
  • Small web design agencies: $3,000 – $10,000+
  • Large agencies: $10,000 – $50,000+

What Affects Website Cost?

  • Number of pages
  • Custom design work
  • Mobile responsiveness
  • Search engine optimization
  • Content and images
  • Hosting and ongoing support

FAQ

How long does it take to build a small business website?

Most projects take about 2–3 weeks depending on how quickly we finalize content and revisions.

Do I need hosting?

Yes. Hosting keeps your website online. I offer hosting and support so you don't have to deal with tech issues.

What do you need from me to start?

Your business name, a short description of what you do, any photos or logo you have, and a few examples of websites you like.

Can I update my website later?

Yes. You can request updates any time, and I offer ongoing support options depending on how often you need changes.

Do you build online stores?

I can, but stores usually require more setup and ongoing management. If you need e-commerce, we'll scope it separately so it's priced correctly.
