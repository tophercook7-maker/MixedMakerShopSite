Why in-home is better than the shop

Computer shops work fine for some things — major hardware repairs, complicated motherboard surgery, data recovery from a dead drive. But for most of what real people actually deal with, the shop model creates more problems than it solves.

You have to unplug your computer, load it into a car, drive it across town, wait three days, get a call you can't hear, come pick it up, and then realize the printer doesn't work anymore at home because the WiFi password changed while you were gone.

In-home is faster, cheaper for most jobs, and avoids the disconnect. We bring tools, sit down at your kitchen table, fix what's wrong, test it in your environment with your printer and your WiFi, and leave when it's actually working. If your daughter's laptop needs the same fix, we look at that too.

We're based in Hot Springs and serve Hot Springs, Hot Springs Village, Lake Hamilton, Lonsdale, Benton, and Malvern. If you're a little further out, ask — we'll usually still come.

Frequently asked questions

Do you come to homes or just businesses?

Both. About half our calls are home users in the Hot Springs area, half are small business owners. Same response time either way.

What if you can't fix it?

If we can't get it working in the time we quoted, you don't pay for that visit. We'll be straight about whether it's worth taking the problem further (sometimes the answer is "the computer is 9 years old, this is the third visit, time for a new one").

Are you a "Geek Squad" type service?

Less corporate, more direct. You're working with Topher — same person who answers the phone, shows up, fixes it, and texts you a week later to make sure it's still working. No mystery techs assigned to your ticket.

Will my data be safe?

Yes. Everything stays on your machine. We don't copy your files to ours. If a fix requires backing up first, we use a drive you own or a folder you control.

Do you remove old computers?

Yes — if you want. We wipe them properly (not just "delete the files") and either return them clean for donation or dispose of them responsibly. Free with any setup of the replacement.
